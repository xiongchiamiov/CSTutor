2.x Previewing a Lesson

This scenario shows how the instructor can preview a lesson. Previewing lessons is important because it allows the instructor to proofread the lesson and look at the layout of the lesson. In this scenario, we assume that the instructor has just logged into the appropriate class and is looking at the admin page. Details of logging in as a admin can be found in Section x. Section 2.6
To preview a lesson, the instructor selects Preview in the toolbar on the admin page. Then, the instructor will be sent to a preview page similiar to what a student sees. Details of browsing the student pages can be found at Section 2.4
